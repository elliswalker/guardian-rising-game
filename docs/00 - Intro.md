# Guardian Rising

> A Kingdom Series-inspired 2D side-scrolling strategy game set in the Destiny universe. Defend the Last City. Find the Guardians. Hold the Light.

---

## Elevator Pitch

You wake up on a dead highway outside the Last City. A Ghost hovers over you, scanning. A blue line pulls the world into view.

*"Eyes up, Guardian."*

You're the Speaker — or close enough. You don't fight. You ride. You build. You find lost Ghosts in the dark corners of the world and use them to summon the Guardians bonded to them. Cayde steps out of a firelit cave. Ikora descends from the shadows. Shaxx charges from the ruins. Each one a champion you can call when the walls start to break.

The Fallen are coming at dusk. The city needs walls. And your Ghost is the only thing keeping you alive.

---

## Core Pillars

| Pillar | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Last City Defense** | Left-to-right strategy. Build walls, recruit defenders, spend Glimmer before the waves hit. |
| **Ghost as Crown** | Your Ghost is your life. If it's captured and carried too far, the tether snaps. "Your Light has been extinguished." New run. |
| **Find the Guardians** | Explore each world to discover elemental Ghosts that summon named Guardians with class-specific supers. |
| **Glimmer Economy** | Hunt, cut, farm, and scavenge to fund the city. Enemies drop it too. Spend wisely. |
| **Pixel Art World** | Destiny 1 color palette in a silhouette-heavy Kingdom-style side scroller. Each planet has its own visual identity and wave cue. |

---

## Influences

- **Kingdom Series** (Noio) — core feel, left/right movement, economy, silent storytelling, defense building
- **Destiny 1** (Bungie) — world, lore, characters, color language, audio direction
