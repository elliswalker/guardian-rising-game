# Ghost Abilities

> The Ghost is your crown. Without it, you have no Light, no Sparrow, no abilities. Treat it accordingly.

---

## How the System Works

1. You start with **Ghost A** — standard, no powers, just Light and companionship
2. Explore each world to find hidden **elemental Ghosts** (glowing caves, dark rifts, secret areas)
3. Unlock with **Legendary Shards + Glimmer**
4. Equipping an elemental Ghost bonds you to that Guardian — their super becomes your double-tap ability
5. Only one Ghost equipped at a time
6. Killing enemies with a super drops **Motes of Light** → collect to reduce cooldown

---

## Cooldown

- Time-based cooldown after each super use
- Motes of Light collected from super kills reduce next cooldown duration
- Ghost captures during cooldown delay: if Ghost is stolen mid-cooldown, cooldown timer pauses

## Ghost Capture Mechanic

1. Specific enemy types can target and grab your Ghost
2. Ghost visually struggles — thrashing animation, pulling toward player
3. Tether stretches as enemy carries Ghost away
4. **Tether snaps** when distance exceeds range → red fade to black → *"Your Light has been extinguished."*
5. While captured: no abilities, no Sparrow, dimmed visibility, player moves on foot
6. Recapturing: kill the carrying enemy before tether snaps
