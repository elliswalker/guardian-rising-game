# Enemy Reference — Pixel Art Guide

> Visual reference for all three enemy factions. Images sourced from destinypedia.com / destiny.wiki.gallery for personal development use.
> Use these as shape/silhouette reference when creating pixel art sprites.

---

## Fallen (Earth — House of Devils)

*Palette: rust, brown, grey, blue-white arc energy. 4-armed (except Dregs). Insectoid limbs, cracked/tattered armor.*

### Dreg — Lowest rank. Two arms (severed as punishment). Weak but fast.
![[Dreg_Grimoire.jpg]]
![[Dreg_ConceptArt.jpg]]
- Two arms only (distinguishes from all other Fallen)
- Wiry, hunched posture
- Shock dagger + pistol
- Dies in 1 headshot — cannon fodder

---

### Vandal — Standard infantry. Four arms. Mid-range combatant.
![[Vandal_Grimoire.jpg]]
![[Vandal_D2Screenshot.jpg]]
- Four arms, taller than Dreg
- House-specific helmet variants (Devils = curved horns, Kings = flat horns + bolt studs)
- Carries shock rifle or wire rifle
- Agile — rapid dodging movement

---

### Captain — Elite. Biggest Fallen infantry. Four arms + arc shield.
![[Captain_Grimoire.jpg]]
- Noticeably taller and wider than Vandal
- "Tattered nobility" bearing — ornate armor
- Arc shield embedded in armor (glows blue)
- Wields cannon, shock blade, or rifle
- High durability

---

### Shank — Flying drone. No legs. Vertical jet engines.
![[Shank_Grimoire.jpg]]
![[Shank_SketchSheet.jpg]]
- Small floating box-shaped robot
- Twin vertical jets for lift
- No weak points, no melee
- Rapid-fire Arc weapons
- House colors on chassis

---

### Servitor — Large floating sphere. AI god-machine.
![[Servitor_Grimoire.jpg]]
- Perfect sphere, roughly human-height in diameter
- Single large glowing purple eye (center)
- Floats and rotates in place
- No visible limbs
- Newer variants: exposed circuitry, degraded plating

## Hive (Moon — Hidden Swarm / Blood of Oryx)

*Palette: black, green, bone-white. Chitin armor, exposed bone structure, glowing green energy.*

### Thrall — Smallest, fastest, weakest. Pure melee rush.
![[Thrall_Grimoire.jpg]]
- Skeletal appearance despite having skin
- No visible eyes (3 faint glowing dots where eyes would be)
- Hunched, almost animalistic posture
- Clawed hands — melee only
- Swarm in large numbers

---

### Acolyte — Main ranged infantry. Middle of Hive hierarchy.
![[Acolyte_Grimoire.jpg]]
![[Acolyte_3DRender.jpg]]
- Taller than Thrall, upright posture
- Exposed chitin/bone plating
- Carries void/solar/arc weapons + grenades
- Main attack force of any Hive encounter

---

### Knight — Elite heavy infantry. Massive armor. Sword + shield.
![[Knight_Grimoire.jpg]]
![[Knight_3DRender.jpg]]
![[Knight_Render.jpg]]
- Significantly larger than Acolyte
- Horned helmet, tattered cape, glowing sigil on face
- Armor "as hard as relic iron"
- Wields Boomer (Arc cannon), Cleaver (sword), or Splinter (crossbow)
- Can deploy Wall of Darkness shield (defensive barrier)
- Surprisingly agile for their size

---

### Wizard — Flying spellcaster. Long robes. Aerial.
![[Wizard_Grimoire.jpg]]
- Floats/flies overhead — never ground-based
- "Long, crumbling robes" flowing behind
- Ranged magic attacks from above
- Female Hive

---

### Ogre — Giant ritual beast. Ground-shaking. Eye-beam.
![[Ogre_Grimoire.jpg]]
- Much larger than Knight — biggest standard Hive unit
- Hunched, massive frame
- Fires energy blasts from its single large eye
- Ground slam when enemies get close
- Enters berserk charge state when wounded

---

## Cabal (Mars — Sand Eaters / Dust Giants / Red Legion)

*Palette: dusty red, military grey, gold accents. Bipedal rhino-like. Heavy armor, jump jets.*

### Legionary — Standard infantry. Largest basic infantry of all factions.
![[Legionary_Grimoire.jpg]]
- Huge bipedal soldiers in full heavy armor
- Jump jets built into armor (can reposition rapidly)
- Slug rifle or projection rifle + wrist blades (melee)
- "Largest and most durable basic infantry from all enemy races"

---

### Phalanx — Shield infantry. Big deployable shield.
![[Phalanx_Grimoire.jpg]]
- Same Cabal build as Legionary
- Carries massive tactical shield covering most of the body
- Exposed arms are weak spot
- D2 Red Legion variants have jump jets + weak spot on back

---

### Centurion — Officer. Jump jets + solar shield. Ranged commander.
![[Centurion_Grimoire.jpg]]
![[Centurion_Render.png]]
- Heavier armor than Legionary, more ornate
- Solar shield embedded in armor
- Jetpack — can hover and boost across battlefield
- Battlefield sensor arrays on helmet
- Commands infantry units

---

### Colossus — Biggest infantry. Rotary cannons. Near-impenetrable.
![[Colossus_Grimoire.jpg]]
- "Massive, even for Cabal standards"
- Rotary machine gun + slug launcher
- Near-impenetrable heavy armor
- Ground-shaking stomps

---

### Psion — Small psychic. Only non-Cabal in Cabal forces.
![[Psion_Grimoire.jpg]]
![[Psion_ConceptArt.jpg]]
- Bipedal humanoid, shorter than humans
- Hairless, veined head
- Single eye with Y-shaped pupil, surrounded by black trident-shaped creases
- Holes above mouth (spiracles for breathing)
- 4 fingers per hand
- Psychic abilities — can split into copies of themselves

---

### Incendior — Flamethrower. Twin fuel tanks on back. Weak spot = tanks.
![[Incendior_Info.jpg]]
![[Incendior_ConceptArt.jpg]]
- Standard Cabal build
- Two large fuel tanks strapped to the back (critical weak point)
- Reinforced helmet — immune to headshots
- Flamethrower weapon
- Strafe thrusters for limited flight
