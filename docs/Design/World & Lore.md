# World & Lore

> Destiny-inspired world-building reference. All factions, locations, and narrative hooks.

---

## Setting

The last safe city on Earth sits beneath the Traveler. The City's walls hold — for now.

## Factions (Enemy Progression)

| Faction | Home Planet | Visual Theme | Threat Style |
|---------|-------------|--------------|-------------|
| Fallen | Earth (starting) | Mechanical scavengers | Waves, fast |
| Hive | Moon | Dark organic / ritual | Tanky, swarming |
| Cabal | Mars | Military empire | Heavy armor, siege |

## Locations / Level Progression

1. Earth — Last City (tutorial + early game)
2. Moon — Hellmouth region
3. Mars — Meridian Bay / Cabal territory
4. *Future:* Venus (Vex), Dreadnaught (Taken), etc.

## Key Characters / Lore Hooks

- **The Traveler** — passive presence, visual backdrop above the city
- **The Speaker** — NPC advisor at the Tower, quest/dialogue
- **Ghost** — player's companion, powers the ability system (see [[Ghost Abilities]])
- **The Guardian** — player character, rezzed at game start

## Narrative Arc (MVP)

> Ghost resurrects a fallen Guardian in the ruins outside the City. The City's defenses are crumbling as the Fallen's House of Devils stages an assault. You must rebuild the walls, recruit defenders, and hold the line.
