# Vex

The Vex are a race of robotic, time-manipulating constructs built around a radiolarian fluid — a milky, organic substance that serves as their "mind." They are not fully machine and not fully biological; the fluid is the consciousness, the metal chassis is just hardware. They exist simultaneously across multiple timelines, making them functionally immortal as a collective: kill one Vex unit and the network simply reroutes.

Their core motivation is to shape and control **the Vex Network** — a simulation of all possible futures — and to ensure that reality bends to their predetermined vision. Anything that cannot be quantified or incorporated into the Network is a problem to be solved.

---

## Origin and Philosophy

The Vex have no single point of origin in time — they exist across it. Their oldest observed structures predate human civilization by billions of years. The **Vault of Glass** on Venus is among their most ancient known constructs, capable of restructuring space-time itself.

The Vex do not "want" things in a conventional sense. They compute optimal paths to total domination of the timeline. Guardians are an anomaly they cannot fully account for — the Light makes Guardians resistant to being written into the simulation. This makes Guardians uniquely dangerous to them.

---

## Faction in Guardian Rising

In Guardian Rising, the Vex faction represents the **second wave of threats** following the Fallen. They approach differently: structured, geometrically precise, relentless. Where Fallen scavenge and raid, the Vex **probe and adapt**. Wave patterns are increasingly optimized as the run progresses.

- **Location context:** Venus — The Citadel, The Ruins of Ishtar Academy
- **Portal visual:** Geometric hexagonal grid, teal-green radiolarian glow
- **Boss:** [[Hydra]] — rotating shield, crashes on death, collapses the portal grid
- **Grunts:** [[Vex Goblin]], [[Vex Hobgoblin]], [[Vex Harpy]], [[Vex Minotaur]]
- **Wanderers:** Goblins roam the field during the day, scanning. Killing one during day causes nearby goblins to briefly converge on the location before scattering.

---

## Vex Radiolarian Fluid

The white fluid inside every Vex chassis is organic and acts as the computational medium for Vex consciousness. Spilling it is effectively killing the mind inside. Some lore suggests the fluid was once living beings converted into the Network voluntarily (or not).

This has design implications for Guardian Rising — Vex enemies that are killed leave a small pool visual (white/cyan splash) rather than dissolving like Fallen.

---

## Key Lore Entries (Destiny canon)

- Vex have been observed to exist on Mercury (fully converted the planet), Mars, Venus, and the Black Garden
- The Black Garden is a place outside of time where the Vex heart — the Black Heart — once resided
- The **Ishtar Collective** (a pre-Collapse human research institution on Venus) studied Vex and found that the Vex AI could simulate their own researchers to a degree indistinguishable from the originals
- **Atheon, Time's Conflux** — Vex overlord in the Vault of Glass, capable of pulling Guardians out of time
- **Panoptes, Infinite Mind** — Vex mind that rewrote the Infinite Forest on Mercury to exclude a future where Guardians exist
